My Debut book

Whispers of a Nyctophile

The human heart is the birthplace of emotions-manifold, that look for outlets to come out from time to time. “Whispers of a nyctophile” is that piece of my heart bringing out a plethora of emotions-of love, heartbreak, strength, nostalgia, harsh realities plaguing the world, unheard voices of people and beauty of nature- all under one roof. It comprises of poetries people will relate to, poetries that speak of things people will reflect upon and poetries of people whose voices go unheard. It is my gift of poetry to all those souls who believe in the power of words and conceive staggering art out of raw emotions in its pristine form.

About me!

Hey there! I am an Indian writer ,poet , robotics CAD designer and musician .I made my debut as an author with my poetry book "Whispers of a Nyctophile" published on 31st August,2020. I am also the co- author of another 8 beautiful collections of poetry. "Whispers of a Nyctophile" made it to the Amazon Best sellers list and got nominated for LITFEST 2020-21. I am a nominee for the prestigious "Author of the Year" award in LITFEST 2021-21 for the same book. My co-authored book "Days and Nights" also made it to Amazon's best seller list. Passionate about reading and writing since primary school, I am also the winner of many literary contests and has my works published in National and International magazines and writing platforms. I am from from Assam, India and I am currently pursuing my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai. I am a robotics enthusiast, I also work as a robotics design engineer, designing and manufacturing robots for National and International championships representing my University along with my team.
